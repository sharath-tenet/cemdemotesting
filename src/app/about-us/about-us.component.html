WHO ARE WE?

Centre For Excellence In Medical Services Private Limited (CEMS) is a Company incorporated on Jan 20, 2017 with an object of establishing high quality Diagnostic Laboratories across India under the Brand Name of “Tenet Diagnostics”.

PURPOSE

Tenet Diagnostics has identified and intends to fulfil the unmet needs of the market by focusing on Quality, Reach, Technology & Logistics and most of all. Customer Experience.

QUALITY

Tenet Diagnostics is on the path of building partnerships with world class established and emerging diagnostics equipment providers, Academic/ Research Institutes, Biotech and Healthcare firms. These partnerships will help the business to stay at the forefront of diagnostic breakthroughs and in adopting them to create quality excellence. These would be backed by getting accredited from quality agencies like NABL & CAP.

WHAT DO WE STAND FOR?

Tenet Diagnostic vertical is envisioned to be a world class diagnostic chain focused on providing high quality diagnostic services to its customers. To establish a state of the art comprehensive diagnostics care unit which will provide services that can cater to every section of the community .

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

create an appropriate platform for R&D where innovative products can be developed which will aid in precise diagnosis of most common healthcare dilemmas.

EDUCATION AND TRAINING

To provide adequate training & development to radiologists, technologists, Engineers in collaboration with prestigious educational institutions and to leverage the abundant, qualified manpower available in the country and to generate gainful employment.

QUALITY CLINICAL IMAGING

Implementing the cutting edge technologies in diagnosing very early stage diseases and to accelerate imaging with a high throughput.

QUALITY LABORATORY SERVICES

Incorporating contemporary best practices in laboratory management with consistent quality and innovation enabling precise diagnosis of some of the most complex diseases.

CLINICAL TRIALS

Collaboration with international companies to perform clinical trials. Consultancy services for healthy life style with conventional and non-conventional lifestyle modification practices.
